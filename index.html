About

Welcome to SpacePulse India, your ultimate resource for real-time information about India's space program, spearheaded by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO). Our mission is to connect citizens with the wonders of space exploration and foster a passion for space technology among the youth of India.

At SpacePulse India, we envision a future where every citizen is aware of India's contributions to space exploration and feels empowered to pursue careers in this dynamic field. By sharing real-time data, updates on ongoing missions, and educational resources, we aim to ignite curiosity and encourage the next generation of space scientists, engineers, and innovators.
